# NeuroCalm - Store Assets

## App Name
NeuroCalm

## Short Description
Emotional regulation for autistic users (ASD). Real-time guided protocols.

## Long Description
NeuroCalm is a specialized emotional regulation app designed specifically for autistic users (ASD). It provides real-time, guided intervention protocols to help manage sensory overload, emotional crises, and daily stress.

Built on the principle of zero cognitive load, NeuroCalm guides you through fixed protocols based on your current emotional state:
- **Leve (Light discomfort):** Gentle breathing exercises to prevent tension buildup.
- **Sobrecarga (Sensory Overload):** Grounding techniques (5-4-3-2-1 method) to reconnect with the environment.
- **Crise (Emotional Crisis):** High-focus countdowns and rhythmic breathing to regain control.

Features:
- **Guided Protocols:** No decisions needed during use. Just follow the rhythm.
- **Communication Tools:** Quick, editable messages for when speaking is difficult.
- **Daily Routine:** Simple task list to maintain structure.
- **Voice Guidance:** Optional auditory prompts to assist focus.
- **Premium Support:** Unlock advanced protocols for deeper regulation.
